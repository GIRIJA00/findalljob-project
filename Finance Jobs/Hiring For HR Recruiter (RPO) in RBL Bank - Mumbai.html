RBL Bank, formerly known as Ratnakar Bank Limited, is a private sector bank in India. It was founded in 1943 and is headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. RBL Bank offers a wide range of banking products and financial services to individuals, businesses, and corporates. The bank operates through various business segments, including Corporate & Institutional Banking, Commercial Banking, Retail Banking, Agribusiness Banking, Development Banking, and Financial Markets.

RBL Bank has a network of branches and ATMs spread across different parts of India. It provides services such as savings accounts, current accounts, fixed deposits, loans, credit cards, wealth management, insurance, and digital banking solutions.

RBL Bank has undergone significant growth and expansion over the years and has become one of the prominent private sector banks in India, serving millions of customers across the country.
